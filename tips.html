Tips/Things to expect for your first try out



-coaches expect for you to be on time and ready to play after warming up.
-other players that are ready to try out will not be nice to you.
-you will be put in matches immediately.
-depending on your skill level you will be moved to play with people similar to you.
-coaches have a diffrent mind set on how he wants his team to play or certian players on his team
-worry about how you play and not others
-show them your skills and show coaches why you deserve to play for them.
-lasty you might not make the team so dont let your self down there are always other clubs.
